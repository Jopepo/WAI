# WAI

WAI is a utility app for airline crew.

It helps calculate wake-up and pickup times from UTC departure times, station time zones, and transport rules.

## Privacy Policy

WAI does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use advertising, analytics, user accounts, location tracking, or third-party tracking.

WAI works locally on your device to calculate wake-up and pickup times based on selected stations, UTC departure times, time zones, and transport rules.

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through the App Store. WAI does not receive, process, or store payment information.
