# WAI 2.2 Legacy Overview

This page documents the current WAI 2.2 public app and its legacy privacy
statement. WAI 3 has separate release, privacy, and operational documents so the
new secure branch does not inherit outdated wording.

## WAI 2.2

WAI 2.2 is a utility app for airline crew.

It helps calculate wake-up and pickup times from UTC departure times, station
time zones, and transport rules.

## Privacy Policy

WAI 2.2 does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use advertising, analytics, user accounts, location tracking,
or third-party tracking.

WAI 2.2 works locally on your device to calculate wake-up and pickup times
based on selected stations, UTC departure times, time zones, and transport
rules.

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through the App Store. WAI 2.2 does not
receive, process, or store payment information.

Effective date: June 2026
